Catering

We offer a number of catering options for groups as small as 10 people to groups as large as 250 people.

Please note that we advise a minimum of one week's notice for larger catering requests; availability is limited. Orders for 10 to 20 guests require a minimum of 72 hours notice.

Options start as low as $15 per person and include an array of bite sized treats.

Custom packages also available; feel free to mix, match, and customize your treats. We'll go above and beyond to make your special day special!

Family Special

Looking to celebrate a birthday, anniversary, or special event with a small group of loved ones? We're holding a limited time special, just for you! For $10 a person, we'll deliver one 8" Round Cake and 2 Dozen bite sized treats of your choice.*

*Add on options and extras may incur an additional fee.
