About Us

Josie's Cupcakery was founded by none other than Grandma Josie, our Grand Master "Cupcake‐a‐teer"! Josie loves what she does, and pours that love into each and every creation that comes out of the kitchen. We're a family business; Jane, Jack, and Emily are Josie's beloved children, and all three of them are a part of the Cupcakery Kitchen. Lois and Tina are Josie's granddaughters, and they too, are a valuable part of the team! Jane and Jack both hold a Bachelors Degree in Culinary Arts, while Emily holds a degree in Culinary Management. Josie's recipes have been featured in three cookbooks to date, and she's currently working on her very first, on her own!

We're a small, but dedicated group ‐ and we promise you won't be disappointed in our products. If you're not satisfied, we're not satisfied, and we'll do everything in our power to make it right!
